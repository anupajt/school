ABOUT US

In the year 1978 Sabarigiri School began its meritorious journey with its vision to educate the future generation of our country, transforming them to very useful citizens under the guidance of our founder chairman Dr. V. K. Jayakumar.

Sabarigiri English School, Anchal is the first CBSE School in Kollam District. Since its inception in 1978-79, the school has made giant strides in the field of quality CBSE school education from kindergarten to Senior Secondary level which reflects on the worldwide network of alumni students holding prestigious posts and leading organizations in various fields since passing out. Being a boarding school, students are given homely care and every attempt is made to make them fine individuals useful to their family and the society around and the nation. The final slogan they carry from school is wherever you may be, let the word to be better on account of you. Our school works with the aspiration and commitment to provide an enjoyable and enriching educational experience to children.
